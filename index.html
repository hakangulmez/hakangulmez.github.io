Research

Generative AI as a Task Shock: Product-Level LLM Substitution in B2B Software Markets
In Progress Master's Thesis · TU Munich · 2026
Difference-in-differences study across NYSE/Nasdaq-listed B2B software firms (SIC 7370–7379), estimating revenue impact of LLM task replicability. Treatment variable constructed via LLM-as-judge scoring of pre-shock product page text using a 10-criteria rubric grounded in Eloundou et al. (2024), Acemoglu & Restrepo (2022), and Brynjolfsson et al. (2023). Preliminary β ≈ −0.60 (p < 0.01); quantity channel dominant; 1.52× intensification from early to advanced AI era. Repository · Companion dashboard.
Does the Existing Technology Stock Accelerate New Invention? Evidence from 10,000 Years of General Purpose Technologies
In Progress Working Paper · 2026
GDP growth panel regression across 30 General Purpose Technologies spanning 10,000 BCE–2017. Key test: does higher accumulated technology stock amplify the growth payoff of new GPT arrivals? Interaction term β₃ = 11.81 (p = 0.048), robust across 100% of leave-one-out iterations. Repository.
Determinants of Technology Diffusion Speed: Endogenous λ Estimation Across 15 Technologies and 132 Countries
In Progress Working Paper · 2026
Logistic adoption curves fit to the CHAT dataset (843 technology-country pairs, median R² = 0.987) to recover technology-specific diffusion speeds (λ). Second stage regresses λ on human capital, predecessor adoption, trade openness, and institutional quality. Predecessor adoption β = 0.516 (p < 0.001, technology FE) — consistent with the technology-stock feedback mechanism. Repository.

Assessing Lockdown Impacts on PM2.5 Levels: Evidence from Italy and Japan
Research Paper · 2025
Daily city-level panel combining air pollution, meteorological, and lockdown stringency data. Difference-in-differences with fixed effects and nearest-neighbor propensity score matching; stronger pollution declines under lockdown in Italy, validated with parallel-trends and placebo tests.
